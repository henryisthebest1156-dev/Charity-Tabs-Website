Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Charity Tabs uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate our extension and display third-party advertisements. These cookies help us track usage, calculate donations, and ensure our extension functions properly.

What cookies do we use?

  • Necessary Cookies: These allow the extension to track your tab count and streaks locally. No personal information is collected.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Our advertising partners may use cookies or web beacons to display ads and measure ad performance. These cookies do not collect personal information.

Managing Cookies

You can clear or block cookies at any time by clearing your browser data. Note that doing so may reset your tab counts and streaks in the extension.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our cookie usage, please contact us at contact@charitytabs.app.
