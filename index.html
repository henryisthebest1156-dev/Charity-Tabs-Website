How It Works

Three simple steps. No cost to you.

  1. 1

    Open a new tab

    Use Chrome as usual. Each new tab is a chance to give back.

  2. 2

    See a simple dashboard with an ad

    Your new tab shows a clean layout and a small, respectful ad.

  3. 3

    Revenue from ads is donated to charity

    Ad earnings fund donations to the causes you care about.

Features

  • Donation tracking

    Watch your impact grow as you browse.

  • Choose your charity

    Pick the organization you want to support.

  • Daily streaks

    Build a habit of giving, one tab at a time.

  • 100% free

    No fees from us—just your normal browsing.

Transparency

We track donations in aggregate and plan to share totals publicly so you can see the community’s impact. We’re committed to clear reporting and honest numbers.

About

Charity Tabs is a Chrome extension that replaces your new tab page with a simple dashboard. Optional ads generate revenue that we donate to charity. We’re building something small, honest, and easy to use.
